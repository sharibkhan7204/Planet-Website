• Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest planet.
  • It is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium.
  • Jupiter has a Great Red Spot, a giant storm lasting centuries.
  • The planet has strong winds and powerful storms across its atmosphere.
  • Jupiter has at least 79 moons, including the large moon Ganymede.
  • Its magnetic field is the strongest of all planets in space.
  • Jupiter rotates very fast, completing one day in about 10 hours.
  • It has faint ring systems made of dust and small particles.
