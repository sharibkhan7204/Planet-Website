• Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun.
  • It has extremely hot days and freezing cold nights.
  • Mercury has a rocky surface covered with craters like the Moon.
  • It orbits the Sun faster than any other planet in space.
  • Mercury has almost no atmosphere to trap heat or protect it.
  • Its year is only 88 Earth days long.
  • Mercury rotates very slowly, causing long days and nights.
  • The planet has a strong metal-rich core creating a magnetic field.
