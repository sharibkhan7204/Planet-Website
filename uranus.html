• Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and an ice giant.
  • It has a pale blue color due to methane in its atmosphere.
  • Uranus rotates on its side, unlike any other planet known.
  • Its atmosphere contains hydrogen, helium, and icy methane clouds.
  • Uranus has faint rings made of dark dust and small particles.
  • The planet has extremely cold temperatures, often below minus 200 degrees.
  • Uranus has 27 known moons named after Shakespeare and Pope characters.
  • It takes 84 Earth years for Uranus to complete one orbit.
