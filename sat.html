• Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and a gas giant.
  • It is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.
  • Saturn’s atmosphere contains hydrogen, helium, and traces of other gases.
  • The planet has more than 80 moons orbiting around it.
  • Titan, Saturn’s largest moon, has thick atmosphere and liquid methane lakes.
  • Saturn has very fast winds, creating banded patterns in its clouds.
  • Its density is so low it could float in a giant ocean.
  • Saturn takes about 29 Earth years to complete one orbit.
