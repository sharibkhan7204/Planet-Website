• Venus is the second planet from the Sun and Earth’s closest neighbor.
  • It has a thick atmosphere made mostly of carbon dioxide gas.
  • Venus experiences the strongest greenhouse effect, making it extremely hot.
  • Its surface temperature is hotter than Mercury despite being farther.
  • Venus rotates very slowly and in the opposite direction.
  • The planet’s surface is covered with volcanoes, plains, and mountains.
  • Thick clouds of sulfuric acid completely hide Venus’s surface from view.
  • Venus is often called Earth’s sister planet due to similar size.
