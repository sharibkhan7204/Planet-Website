• Pluto is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt region.
  • It was once considered the ninth planet before reclassification in 2006.
  • Pluto has a thin atmosphere that freezes and disappears when far.
  • Its surface contains nitrogen ice, methane ice, and rocky materials.
  • Pluto has five known moons, with Charon being the largest companion.
  • It has a heart-shaped region called Tombaugh Regio on its surface.
  • Pluto orbits the Sun very slowly, taking 248 Earth years.
  • New Horizons spacecraft provided detailed images and discoveries about Pluto.
